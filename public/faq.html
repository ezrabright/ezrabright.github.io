Engagement FAQs

  • What does EzraBright Consulting specialise in? We deliver Data Engineering and Business Intelligence services, including data foundations, warehousing, integration, and executive reporting.
  • Which organisations do you work with? We support teams across finance, operations, and leadership functions, with engagements tailored to business needs and reporting maturity.
  • Do you work with clients outside Johannesburg? Yes. We support clients across South Africa through remote delivery and can arrange on-site sessions when required.

  • How do we start? Submit a request via the website form or email info@ezrabright.co.za. We will schedule a discovery session and confirm scope.
  • How long do projects typically take? Timelines vary by scope. After discovery, we provide a delivery plan with clear milestones and timelines.
  • Do you provide documentation and handover? Yes. We provide structured documentation, handover sessions, and governance guidance where applicable.

  • Which tools do you use? Power BI, SQL, and modern data engineering tooling aligned to the client environment (on-prem, hybrid, or cloud).
  • Can you work with Excel and legacy systems? Yes. We routinely migrate and integrate Excel/CSV and legacy sources into structured SQL environments for governed reporting.

  • How is pricing determined? Pricing is based on scope, delivery approach, timelines, and complexity. We provide a clear proposal after discovery.
  • Do you provide ongoing support? Yes. Ongoing support can be arranged through a structured support and enhancement agreement.
  • How do scope changes work? Scope changes are documented and agreed in writing through a formal change process.
