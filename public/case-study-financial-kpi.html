Automated Financial Reporting Dashboard

Reducing financial reporting time by 60% with automated dashboards

Overview

A financial services company lacked visibility into key performance indicators, limiting their ability to track performance, risks, and profitability.

Challenges

  • KPIs scattered across multiple systems.
  • Manual calculations caused errors and delays.
  • Difficulty in monitoring trends and predicting performance.

Solution

  • Designed centralized financial KPI dashboards using BI tools.
  • Automated KPI calculations and visualizations.
  • Provided predictive analytics to forecast trends and risks.

Results

  • Improved KPI tracking accuracy by 50%.
  • Enhanced decision-making for executives and managers.
  • Enabled faster response to financial risks and opportunities.

Key Metrics

KPI accuracy: +50%
Risk detection improvement: +35%
Executive adoption: 100%
