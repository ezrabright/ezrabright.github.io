Automated Financial Reporting Dashboard

Reducing financial reporting time by 60% with automated dashboards

Overview

A mid-sized business faced inefficiencies due to repetitive manual processes across finance, HR, and operations.

Challenges

  • Time-consuming manual workflows.
  • High risk of errors and inconsistencies.
  • Lack of centralized visibility into process performance.

Solution

  • Implemented automated workflows using BI and RPA (Robotic Process Automation) tools.
  • Centralized data to monitor workflow efficiency in real-time dashboards.
  • Set up proactive alerts for process bottlenecks and errors.

Results

  • Reduced manual effort by 40%.
  • Errors in reporting and operations decreased by 35%.
  • Increased process visibility and accountability.

Key Metrics

Workflow automation efficiency: +40%
Error reduction: -35%
Process visibility: 100%
