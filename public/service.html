Business Intelligence Consulting

Delivering strategic BI solutions that empower data-driven decision-making, including Power BI development, advanced data modeling, SQL optimization, ETL pipelines, and BI architecture frameworks.

Data Analysis & Actionable Insights

Transforming raw data into clear insights for operations, finance, and strategy through trend and KPI analysis, root-cause investigation, performance dashboards, forecasting, and custom reports.

Reporting & Dashboard Automation
