Our Services

EzraBright Consulting is a Data Engineering and Business Intelligence firm. We build reliable data foundations and deliver executive-ready insights through dashboards, KPI reporting, and automated reporting systems.

Data Engineering

We design and build scalable data pipelines that move, clean, and structure data from your source systems into analysis-ready datasets. This includes extraction, transformation, validation, and scheduling.

Data Warehousing & Modelling

We design data warehouse structures and dimensional models that make reporting faster, consistent, and trusted. Perfect for finance, operations, and executive reporting.

ETL & Systems Integration

We integrate data across platforms (databases, Excel/CSV, ERP/accounting systems, APIs) so your reporting reflects one source of truth. We reduce manual consolidation and improve accuracy.

Business Intelligence & Power BI

We build executive dashboards and operational performance reports that track KPIs, trends, and targets. Our dashboards are designed for clear decision-making, not just visuals.

KPI Reporting & Automation

We replace manual reporting with automated reporting packs and scheduled refreshes. This includes KPI definitions, metric consistency, and executive-ready reporting outputs.

SQL & Database Optimisation

We optimise SQL queries, improve database performance, and implement data quality checks to ensure fast dashboards and reliable reporting. Ideal for growing datasets and reporting workloads.

Cloud Data Enablement (Analytics Focused)

We support Azure data platform advisory, cloud BI deployment, and hybrid architectures (on-prem + cloud) specifically for analytics and reporting. This is not “full infrastructure migration” — it’s focused on enabling reporting platforms and data pipelines.

Our Services

EzraBright Consulting is a specialist Data Engineering and Business Intelligence firm. We architect enterprise-grade data foundations and deliver decision-intelligence platforms that enable leadership teams to operate with clarity, control, and confidence.

Data Engineering & Architecture

We design and implement scalable data architectures that consolidate disparate systems into structured, governed, and analytics-ready environments. Our capabilities include data pipeline engineering, transformation frameworks, validation controls, and enterprise data modelling to ensure reliability and long-term scalability.

Data Warehousing & Enterprise Modelling

We develop structured data warehouse environments aligned to business performance measurement. Through dimensional modelling and subject-area design, we enable consistent KPI definitions, trusted reporting outputs, and cross-functional visibility across finance and operations.

Systems Integration & Data Consolidation

We integrate ERP, accounting, operational databases, spreadsheets, and API-driven systems into a unified reporting ecosystem. Our structured ETL frameworks eliminate manual consolidation, enhance data accuracy, and establish a single source of truth across the organisation.

Business Intelligence & Executive Reporting

We design executive dashboards and performance intelligence platforms that transform data into strategic insight. Our BI solutions provide leadership with real-time KPI visibility, trend analysis, financial performance tracking, and board-ready reporting aligned to business objectives.

Reporting Automation & Performance Governance

We replace fragmented manual reporting processes with automated, controlled, and standardised reporting environments. This includes metric governance, scheduled data refreshes, automated reporting packs, and structured performance frameworks that ensure consistency and executive trust.

Database Development & Optimisation

We design, create, and optimise SQL-based database environments that support high-performance reporting workloads. Our services include database creation, migration from legacy systems, data quality controls, indexing strategies, and performance tuning to ensure scalable and reliable analytics delivery.

Cloud Data Enablement & Platform Advisory

We provide advisory and implementation support for analytics-focused cloud environments, including Azure data platforms, hybrid architectures, and cloud-based BI deployment. Our approach prioritises structured migration of reporting and analytics workloads—ensuring scalability, governance, and long-term technology alignment without unnecessary infrastructure complexity.
