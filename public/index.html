About EzraBright Consulting

EzraBright Consulting is a South African Data Engineering and Business Intelligence firm. We help organisations consolidate data across systems, establish trusted reporting foundations, and deliver executive-ready insights through governed dashboards and automated KPI reporting.

Our work spans data architecture, warehousing and modelling, integration pipelines, database development, and Power BI enablement. We focus on measurable outcomes: reduced manual reporting, improved data quality, faster decision cycles, and scalable reporting environments built for growth.

Our Engagement Approach

Our structured methodology ensures that every engagement delivers measurable impact, scalable architecture, and long-term performance visibility.

1. Discovery & Assessment

We begin with a structured consultation to understand your systems, reporting challenges, and strategic objectives. This includes reviewing current data sources, reporting workflows, and performance measurement frameworks.

2. Architecture & Solution Design

Based on the assessment, we design a tailored data architecture and reporting framework. This may include database design, data warehouse modelling, integration pipelines, KPI definitions, and dashboard strategy aligned to leadership needs.

3. Implementation & Performance Enablement

We implement the approved solution, deploy dashboards and reporting automation, and ensure governance controls are in place. The outcome is a structured, scalable, and decision-ready analytics environment.
